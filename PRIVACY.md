# Privacy Policy for Note Enough

**Last updated:** March 18, 2026

## Overview

Note Enough is an iPadOS note-taking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is handled.

## Data Collection

**Note Enough does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The app operates entirely offline on your device. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.

## Data Storage

All data created within Note Enough — including your notebooks (stacks), pages, and drawings — is stored locally on your iPad using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device through the app.

Specifically, the app stores:

- **Notebook metadata:** Names, paper type, paper size, orientation, creation dates, and display order.
- **Drawing data:** Your handwritten notes and drawings encoded as PencilKit data.
- **App preferences:** Your chosen defaults for paper type, size, orientation, navigation direction, and deletion confirmation setting. These are stored in standard UserDefaults on your device.

## Third-Party Services

Note Enough does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

## Internet Access

Note Enough does not require or use an internet connection. The app functions entirely offline.

## Data Sharing

When you use the built-in share/export feature, your notes are converted to a PDF file and presented through the standard iPadOS share sheet. Any sharing that occurs from that point is initiated and controlled by you through the operating system, not by the app.

## Children's Privacy

Note Enough does not collect any data from any user, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## iCloud and Backups

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPad, your Note Enough data may be included in your device backup as part of the standard iOS backup process. This is managed by Apple and your device settings, not by the app.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date. Because the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer through the App Store listing.
