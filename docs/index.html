Note Enough

Focus on your thoughts, not the UI. A minimalist iPadOS note-taking app designed for precision and digital organization.

View on App Store Requires iPadOS 17.0 or later
📚

The Heap

Organize all your digital notebooks in a clean, grid-based home screen for instant access.

🖊️

Precision Drawing

Leverage the full power of Apple Pencil and PencilKit with native tools and zero latency.

📂

Digital Stacks

Configure paper type, size, and orientation. Your drawings persist automatically via SwiftData.

Designed for iPad

  • Full PencilKit: Use the standard tool palette with full pressure and tilt sensitivity.
  • Configurable Stacks: Choose between Plain, Textured, or Graph paper styles.
  • Intuitive Navigation: Swipe naturally between pages with configurable scroll directions.
  • Modern Persistence: Powered by SwiftData for reliable, efficient storage.
